Frequently Asked Questions

can you print a document from your laptop at Hz?
Yes you can! you first need to go to this page, log onto your account and upload all the files you wish to print. Then, you go to the printer (e.g on the first floor next to the staircase), scan your card, choose printing option and your documents should be printed..

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
Go to the webshop and log in to your account. After, you can purchase what you want you can even pay with PayPal.
How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
You can book it from: self service portal then go to 'Make a reservation', after that 'Reserve meeting room or Project Group room' and 'Reserve a room' and then you can choose a room.
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
reveal your HZ pass at the barriers. After that, you can park in the lot.
